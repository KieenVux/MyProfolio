# Functional Map

![Functional Map](../images/functional_map.png)

## Overview
The Hamanoya Internal App is divided into two major functional zones: the **Admin Core** for management staff, and the **Employee Portal** for general staff usage.

## 1. Admin Core (Management)

### Content Management Engine
The heart of the application, empowering HR and managers to distribute information.
- **Create Rich Text Content**: Uses an advanced TipTap WYSIWYG editor to format complex documents.
- **Media Uploads**: Attach images and PDF files directly to policies.
- **Categorization**: Group documents logically (e.g., "Crisis Manuals", "Weekly News").
- **Publish & Notify**: Hitting publish immediately triggers the LINE WORKS broadcast module.

### User Management Engine
- **Employee Onboarding**: Create new user profiles using strict internal Employee Codes.
- **RBAC Assignment**: Grant Admin, Manager, or standard User privileges.

### Feedback Management
- **Review Anonymous Opinions**: Safely read complaints or suggestions submitted by employees.
- **Track Status**: Mark feedback as "Seen" or "Actioned" to keep HR workflows organized.

---

## 2. Employee Portal

### View Content
- **Read Company News**: Access the latest corporate announcements.
- **Review Policies & Manuals**: A central digital library for standard operating procedures.
- **Download Attachments**: Access necessary forms or offline manuals.

### Submit Feedback
- **Write Opinions**: A direct channel to upper management.
- **Upload Proof**: Attach images or files to substantiate feedback.
- **Toggle Anonymous Mode**: A critical privacy switch that guarantees backend identity stripping for sensitive whistleblowing.
